Our Story

LifeBridge Church Uganda was founded by Pastor Godfrey Mukwaya in 2022 in the small village of Kajjansi, Wakiso District, Uganda.

What began as a small gathering of believers has grown into a thriving ministry committed to reaching souls, making disciples, planting churches, and transforming communities through the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Today, LifeBridge Church has over 100 registered members worshipping across our branches and actively participating in ministry, evangelism, and community outreach.

Community Impact & Child Education Support

At LifeBridge Church, we believe that every child deserves hope, opportunity, and access to education.

Through our community support program, we help vulnerable children return to school and encourage them to complete their education.

Currently, the church is supporting 10 children with educational assistance and related needs.

We warmly invite individuals, churches, organizations, and well-wishers to partner with us as we continue giving children hope through education, discipleship, and practical support.
